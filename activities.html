Great Activities for Kids

Below are some ideas for activates that you can do with your Autistic child or they can do by them self. Please touch the “Read More” to find out more.

  1. Swimming

    An absolute favourite as it’s cheap and quick to get ready most Autistic kids absolutely love water because of the heighten sensory experience but the reverse is also true some just can’t handle the sensory overload.

    Read More

  2. Drawing and Colouring

    Autistic children find the drawing and or colouring gives them an outlet to express them self that they can find hard by any other way, most autistic kids are very detail orientated and this can lead to some extraordinary art work.

    Read More

  3. Tablet

    Since the arrival of the tablet parents and Autistic professionals have been given the opportunity to engage these kids on a level that is less alien to them , almost any activity can be seen as a game and Autistic kids can interact with lessons to puzzles and many more activates without the human element that they don’t know how to deal with relaxing them.

    Read More

  4. Singing and Dancing

    Singing , music and dancing can all give kids much joy, where this is usually a solo past time for Autistic children as they can find it hard to interact with others making up their own words and dance moves can be very stimulating to them
